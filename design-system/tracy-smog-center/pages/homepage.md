# Homepage Override

This page-specific system overrides the generated Master where the local-business PRD and targeted database searches are more specific.

## Product Fit

- Product type: hyperlocal automotive inspection service.
- Pattern: conversion-optimized hero plus trust and authority.
- Primary task: call the shop. Secondary task: open directions.
- Tone: established, direct, local, practical, and trustworthy; never futuristic, luxury-dealership, racing-themed, or SaaS-like.

## Visual System

- Style: Flat Design + Trust & Authority with restrained block-based accents.
- Primary CTA: Smog Red `#AF1F30` on white text.
- Directions accent: Service Blue `#2F68A6` on white text.
- Primary text/header: Charcoal `#2A2B2D`.
- Surfaces: Clean White `#FFFFFF`, Light Gray `#F2F3F4`, Soft Red `#F8EAEC` for the coupon only.
- Borders: `#D9DDE1`; use crisp 1px dividers and 0-8px radii, not pill-heavy SaaS cards.
- Red is reserved for the call action and coupon emphasis. Blue is reserved for directions, links, and informational details.

## Typography

- Heading: Outfit 700/900, geometric and automotive without aggression.
- Body/UI: Work Sans 400/500/600 for high readability.
- Hero: 48-64px desktop and 40-48px mobile; tight but readable line-height.
- Body: minimum 16px mobile, 1.55-1.7 line-height, 60-75 character desktop measure.
- Buttons: Outfit 700, concise sentence case; avoid ultra-condensed display faces and monospace body text.

## Layout Pattern

1. Utility strip with approved hours and tap-to-call phone.
2. Compact brand header with minimal navigation and a dominant Call Now action.
3. Hero showing the core promise, walk-in message, Call Now, Get Directions, and immediate trust signals.
4. Proof strip: STAR Certified, Test-Only, All Cars, Diesel, Free Retest.
5. Coupon with owner-approval disclaimer.
6. Services and Why Choose Us.
7. Three-step How It Works.
8. FAQ.
9. Reviews link without fabricated testimonials.
10. Location, hours, payments, service areas, directions, and footer.

## Interaction and Responsive Rules

- Mobile-first at 375, 768, 1024, and 1440px.
- Persistent mobile call action may be used only if content padding prevents overlap.
- All touch targets are at least 48px high with 8px separation.
- Focus rings are 3px and clearly visible; no removed outlines.
- Hover/press feedback uses color, shadow, or 1-2px transform in 150-250ms without layout shift.
- Respect `prefers-reduced-motion`; no continuous decorative motion.
- Use semantic links for phone and directions. Do not hide critical content behind accordions except FAQ answers.
- Use official shop photography only when supplied. Until then, use typography, layout, and subtle CSS geometry rather than generic stock vehicles or fabricated storefront imagery.

## Avoid

- SaaS gradients, glassmorphism, excessive pills, dashboard-like panels, racing motifs, luxury-car imagery, dark full-page themes, fabricated certifications, fake review quotes, or guaranteed wait/pass claims.
- Overloading the first viewport. Keep one primary CTA (Call Now) and one clearly subordinate directions action.
